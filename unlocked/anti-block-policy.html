Commitment to Accessibility

At D1 Games, we guarantee that our platform will remain accessible for users as long as it is actively maintained and under development. Should the site be blocked by network restrictions or firewalls, we pledge to swiftly update our URL to bypass such limitations.

Response to Blocking Reports

If D1 Games is reported as blocked in any region, network, or institution, we will take the following action:

  • Verify the accessibility status through internal checks.
  • Update the website URL to restore user access.
  • Notify users of the new access point via public announcements.

Preventive Measures

To avoid detection and preemptive blocking, we employ:

  • Regular domain rotation techniques.
  • Secure traffic routing to minimize visibility.
  • Consistent monitoring of network restrictions.

User Responsibilities

Users are expected to:

  • Report accessibility issues immediately via our official contact channels.
  • Avoid sharing URLs on restricted networks to prevent unnecessary flagging.
  • Use the latest domain provided by our official sources.

Limitations

While we commit to keeping the site unblocked, we cannot guarantee circumvention of all filters, as certain restrictions are beyond our control. However, we will always strive to offer alternative access solutions.
